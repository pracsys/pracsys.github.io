Location



    The PRACSYS lab is located at 1 Spring street in downtown New Brunswick, NJ. If you are visiting and need parking access, please coordinate with Dr. Bekris. The garage is located on the left side of Church street before the intersection with Spring street in downtown New Brunswick. Once you have parked inside the garage, you can walk to the intersection of Spring street and Paterson street. The entrance to our building is on the left side of Spring street (following car traffic) before the intersection with Paterson. It is a glass door marked as Rutgers University. You can take the elevator to the 3rd floor, where the robotics lab and our offices are located.
